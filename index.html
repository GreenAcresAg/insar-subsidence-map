Data Sources

All data shown is from publicly available datasets:

  • TRE ALTAMIRA InSAR Subsidence Data — CA DWR (SGMA technical assistance)
  • DWR SAR Image Services — live ArcGIS REST feed

InSAR vertical displacement is derived from ESA Sentinel-1 radar, processed by TRE ALTAMIRA for the California Department of Water Resources. Values represent average displacement over 100 m × 100 m areas and are measured relative to satellite line-of-sight, converted to vertical. For informational purposes only.
